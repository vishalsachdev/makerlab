Songang Students Visit the MakerLab

Published on Thu, 27 Jun 2013 by AricRindfleisch

Earlier this week, a group of students from Sogang University in Korea visited the MakerLab as part of their Certificate in Technology Management, which is offered through our College's Masters of Science in Technology Management Program. For nearly all of these students, this was the first time seeing 3D printing up close and personal. They were amazed by the possibilities of this new technology and were surprised that a lab such as ours is based in a business school (which is a common reaction among many visitors).
