Private Events

Are you interested in hosting a workshop or event for a private group of individuals? We're happy to schedule events with you outside of our regular workshop times and hours, as our Lab Guru's schedules permit. Our rates for private workshops are the same as our regularly offered workshops at $20 per person for a 90-minute workshop but with a minimum charge of $150.

If you are interested in hosting a private event or workshop at the MakerLab, please contact us. We should respond within 24-48 hours to coordinate with you and help you schedule your event.
