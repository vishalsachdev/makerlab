MakerLab Draws Crowds at MakerFaire

Published on Wed, 25 Sep 2013 by AricRindfleisch

Last Saturday, Aric, Danny, Nick & Vishal represented the MakerLab at the Urbana-Champaign Mini-MakerFaire. We demonstrated 3D printing, displayed many of the objects that we have printed in our lab, and handed out our famous Illinois MakerLab keychains. The event was attended by a large number of families, most of whom had never seen 3D printing in person before. If you missed us at the MakerFaire, please feel free to stop by our lab for a free tour.
