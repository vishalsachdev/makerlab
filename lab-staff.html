Shirley Shah - Guru - Designer

Shirley Shah is a Junior in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. She started volunteering in Fall 2020. She really likes the design aspect of 3D printing and how it opens up a wide variety of practical solutions. Outside of the lab, Shirley is the treasurer for Women in Aerospace and Student Aircraft Builders. In her free time, she really enjoys to paint and play the piano.
