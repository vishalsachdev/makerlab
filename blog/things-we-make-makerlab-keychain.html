Things We Make: Makerlab Keychain

Published on Mon, 25 Mar 2013 by AricRindfleisch

Hot off the press! We are printing our own keychains. This keychain, which was adapted from a design by bradk2012, can be found on our Thingiverse webpage. Just go to: www.thingiverse.com/uimakerlab/designs. They make great gifts and we always have a ready supply in our Lab to hand out to visitors. When we run low, we just print more!
