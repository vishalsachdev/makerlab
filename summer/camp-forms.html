Camp Forms

Required forms for all summer camp participants

Please complete and return the required forms before your camper's first day of camp. Only one set of forms is needed per camper, even if registering for multiple camps.

How to Submit

  • Email: uimakerlab@illinois.edu
  • Mail: Illinois MakerLab, 515 East Gregory Drive, BIF Room 3030, Champaign, IL 61820
  • In Person: Bring completed forms on the first day of camp
