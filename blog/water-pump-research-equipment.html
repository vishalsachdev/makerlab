Custom Lab Equipment: 3D Printed Syringe Holders for Research

Published on Wed, 12 Nov 2025 by Illinois MakerLab

Research often requires specialized equipment that simply doesn't exist commercially. When Md Rashed Al Mahfuz needed custom holders for 30cc syringes as part of a water pump research project, 3D printing provided the perfect solution.

Precision Requirements

Lab equipment needs to be reliable and precise. For this project, the specifications were exacting:

  • 50% infill for maximum strength and durability
  • Grey PLA for visibility in the lab environment
  • Three copies of each part for redundancy
  • No supports needed due to careful design orientation

The higher infill percentage ensures these holders can withstand repeated use in an active research setting.

CFOP-Funded Research

This project was funded through a campus CFOP account, demonstrating how university departments can leverage the MakerLab for research needs. Our pricing for CFOP accounts makes custom fabrication accessible for grant-funded research across campus.

The Value of Custom Fabrication

Commercial lab equipment suppliers often charge premium prices for specialized components, with long lead times. 3D printing offers researchers:

  • Rapid turnaround—days instead of weeks
  • Lower costs for small quantities
  • Ability to iterate and modify designs quickly
  • Complete customization for specific experimental setups

Need custom lab equipment? Faculty and research staff can use CFOP accounts for MakerLab services. Contact us to discuss your project requirements!
