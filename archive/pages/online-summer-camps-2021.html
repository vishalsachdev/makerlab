Online Summer Camps 2021

Learn 3D Printing from Home

Note: This page contains information about our 2021 summer camps. This content is archived for historical reference. For current summer camp information, please contact us.

Guidelines

  • Each camp is limited to 10 participants so that campers get personal attention .

  • All camps are for four days (Monday - Thursday), two hours each day. Morning camps run from 10-12 pm, and afternoon camps from 1-3 pm.

  • You can sign up for both morning and afternoon camps as we avoid duplication of content.

  • ﻿A minimum of 5 campers are needed for a camp to be offered.

  • ﻿All camps will be offered through Zoom.

  • Prints will be placed outside of Business Instructional Facility (BIF) by the Sixth Street entrance when they are ready for pickup. If you wish to have the prints shipped, there will be a $10 surcharge.

  • ﻿All prices listed below exclude the fees charged by Eventbrite, the website we use for bookings. Each camp is listed with the date of the first day, even though it is a four day camp (morning or afternoon). The cost mentioned is for the entire week.

  • Refund Policy: If you cancel…

    • More than 30 Days prior to event - Full Refund

    • 14-30 days prior to event - 50% refund

    • 7-14 days prior to the event - No refund but we offer 50% credit for another event of choice

    • Less than 7 days - No refund

  • ﻿Have a question? Contact us

Adventures in 3D Modeling and Design with TinkerCad

Description:

Creative problem solving at its best! This camp introduces participants to a new world of problem solving. That is the world of 3D printing! Participants will gain a competence in the basics of TinkerCad. Once mastered, participants will use their skills to design an object of their choice using the concepts taught. Students will engage in hands-on and iterative 3D printing activities to demonstrate the knowledge and design skills gained through the camp. Participants will receive their models at the end of the camp.

Ages: 9+

Hours: 2 hours a day – FOUR days a week

Online Summer 2021 Dates:

June 14th to June 17th: 1:00pm - 3:00pm

June 28th to July 1st: 10:00am - 12:00pm

July 12th to July 15th: 1:00pm - 3:00pm

July 26th to July 29th: 10:00am - 12:00pm

Price: $65

Prerequisites: None

Minecraft + 3D Printing

Description:

Join us for an educational and a fun camp for the world of Minecraft! Minecraft camp is a great learning opportunity centered on teaching design, 3D modeling, and printing tools and processes. It provides participants the opportunity to learn creative world-building and the fundamentals of digital modeling principles using a variety of digital prototyping tools. Participants will build their imaginary world in the digital world of Minecraft and receive their 3D printed digital buildings at the end of the camp.

Adventures in 3D Modeling and Design with Fusion360

Description:

Participants in this camp will learn to use Fusion 360 to model and design everyday objects. With a simple to learn interface, Fusion 360 gives designers the tools to succeed in the world of 3D design. Participants will experience designing two separate models in Fusion 360. This camp exposes participants to principles and tools used in professional design. Besides designing, participants will be introduced to common 3D printed concepts. Participants will receive their models at the end of the camp.

Ages: 9+

Hours: 2 hours a day – FOUR days a week
