Make your own Halloween Costume!

Published on Mon, 20 Oct 2014 by AricRindfleisch

Halloween is just around the corner. If you are like us, you are still trying to decide what costume you will wear this year. Store bought costumes are cliche, expensive, and often fit poorly. Fortunately, our Lab can fix this! Using the power of 3D printing, you can create your a customize and unique costume for a fraction of the price that you would pay for a store-bought one. If you already have a costume, our Lab can help you accessorize your Halloween activities by printing pumpkins, jack-o-lanterns, ghosts, and other Halloween-related objects. Your only limits are your imagination!

Come by our lab, make something and if you share it with us on Facebook or Twitter, you get 10% off right then. Hurry fast--our special 10% discount ends on Halloween eve.
