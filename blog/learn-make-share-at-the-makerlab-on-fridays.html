Learn, Make, Share at the MakerLab on Fridays

Published on Wed, 23 Oct 2013 by uimakerlab@illinois.edu

On Fridays from 11:00 to 12:00, the MakerLab will be holding workshops on a variety of digital making topics including, 3D scanning, 3D printing, and how to tinker with open hardware. A list of scheduled workshops is available at /events/ If you have skills in 3D modeling/printing or in working with open hardware (arduinos, raspberry pi's, etc) and would like to offer a workshop, please contact us at UIMakerLab AT illinois DOT edu. Join the Maker movement at Illinois. Subscribe to our blog RSS feed to be the first to hear about events, new tutorials, and other exciting news from the MakerLab.  Or Like our Facebook page to stay connected and follow us on Twitter.
