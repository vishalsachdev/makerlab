Summer Jobs

Due to the new regulations released by the administration and the current situation, the Illinois MakerLab is canceling all June & July 2020 Summer Camps. We apologize for the inconvenience and will look at rescheduling summer camps for August 2020 so be on the lookout for any updates. Please reach out if you have any questions or concerns.

We have two available positions for Summer 2020! Apply to be a volunteer or a guru and help make a kids week at one of our summer camps!

Summer 2020 Intern Volunteer Application

The Illinois MakerLab is seeking creative, innovative teens between 14-17 to help support our summer camps. Our summer camps are a fast paced camps designed to teach attendees the ins & out of 3D Printing. Campers will learn about 3D printing, modeling, designing and more over the course of 5 days! Interns will aid the guru running the camp with mentoring the campers, setting up prints, and ensuring the camps run smoothly.

Camps run twice a day 5 days a week with morning camps happening from 9am - 12pm and afternoon camps between 1-4pm. Volunteer for one session or both, it's completely dependent on the intern's availability. Volunteers with experience in 3D printing or working with younger children appreciated but not needed! We are looking for energetic volunteers with a willingness to learn about the 3D printing revolution & help out! These internships are unpaid, but every intern is eligible for camp fee waivers for themselves or family members. Each intern will get a volunteer certificate signifying the number of hours they volunteered for over the course of the summer.

Summer 2020 Guru Application

The Illinois MakerLab is seeking UIUC students to lead our summer camps. Our summer camps are a fast paced camps designed to teach attendees the ins & out of 3D Printing. Campers will learn about 3D printing, modeling, designing and more over the course of 5 days! Gurus will be tasked with designing lesson plans, mentoring campers in 3D printing, and teaching the essentials of 3D printing, designing and modeling.

Camps run twice a day 5 days a week with morning camps happening from 9am - 12pm and afternoon camps between 1-4pm. Gurus are expected to be able to help out with both camps each week. Previous experience in 3D printing and working with kids is appreciated. Guru positions are paid and are only available to current UIUC students.
