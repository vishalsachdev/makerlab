Summer Camps and Programs

Camp Guidelines

  • All camps are for five days (Monday - Friday), three hours each day. Morning camps run from 9-12 pm, and afternoon camps from 1-4 pm.
  • You can sign up for both morning and afternoon camps as we avoid duplication of content.
  • Additional Info:
    • Early drop off between 8-8:15 am is available for $10
    • Late Pick Up at 5 pm is available for $10
    • If your child is staying through the lunch hour, please pack or order for delivery (see address above) a nut-free meal.
    • Parents can drop kids off starting at 8:30am for no extra charge.
  • A minimum of 3 campers are needed for a camp to be offered. A maximum of eight campers are accepted at each camp.
  • All camps are at Illinois MakerLab, Business Instructional Facility (BIF), Room 3030
  • Please check the age requirement for each camp before registering. In some cases, parents can register as attendees as well.
  • All prices listed below exclude the fees charged by Eventbrite, the website we use for bookings. Each camp is listed with the date of the first day, even though it is a five-day camp (morning or afternoon). The cost mentioned is for the entire week.
  • Please fill out the Waiver forms for minors and bring them with you to the session.
  • Refund Policy: If you cancel...
    • More than 30 Days prior to event - Full Refund less eventbrite fees
    • 14-30 days prior to event - 50% refund less eventbrite fees
    • 7-14 days prior to the event - No refund but we offer 50% credit for another event of choice.
    • Less than 7 days - No refund
  • Have a question? Contact us
  • Contact us for Private Events or Birthday Parties.

Summer Camp Offerings

Adventures in 3D Modeling and Design with Tinkercad

Creative problem-solving at its best! This camp introduces participants to a new world of problem-solving. That is the world of 3D printing! Participants will gain competence in the basics of TinkerCad. Once mastered, participants will use their skills to design an object of their choice using the concepts taught. Students will engage in hands-on and iterative 3D printing activities to demonstrate the knowledge and design skills gained through the camp. Participants will receive their models at the end of the camp.

Ages: 10-15

Hours: 3 hours a day – Five days a week

Schedule: Weeks of June 2/9 at 9 am (Mostly sold out)
Weeks of June 16/23 at 1 pm

Price: Regular Admission: $150

Prerequisites: None

AI and 3D Printing Camp

Prepare your child for the future with this hands-on, cutting-edge workshop that combines Artificial Intelligence (AI) and 3D printing. Young innovators will learn how AI can enhance creativity and engineering, turning their ideas into real, physical objects through digital design and fabrication. By the end of the week, students will bring home a 3D-printed creation of their own design and gain a deeper understanding of how AI is shaping the future of technology and innovation.

Ages: 9+

Hours: 3 hours a day – Five days a week

Schedule: Weeks of July 7/14/21 at 1 pm

Price: Regular Admission: $150

Prerequisites: None

Minecraft Camp

Join us for an educational and a fun camp for the world of Minecraft! Minecraft camp is a great learning opportunity centered on teaching design, 3D modeling, and printing tools and processes. It provides participants the opportunity to learn creative world-building and the fundamentals of digital modeling principles using a variety of digital prototyping tools. Participants will build their imaginary world in the digital world of Minecraft and receive their 3D printed digital buildings at the end of the camp*.

Ages: 9-14

Hours: 3 hours a day – Five days a week

Schedule: Weeks of June 2/9 at 1 pm (Mostly sold out)
Weeks of June 16/23 at 9 am
Weeks of July 7/14/21 at 9 am (New dates added)

Price: Regular Admission: $150

Prerequisites: None

Robotic Arm Camp

Over five engaging days, participants aged 14-18 learn about designing and 3D printing robotic components using Autodesk Fusion 360, while learning to assemble and calibrate their creations. They explore LeRobot's powerful pre-trained models, implementing AI policies for tasks like object manipulation and autonomous sorting. The camp culminates in a showcase where each camper demonstrates how their robotic arm fares in the challenges setup by us.

Each camper takes home a fully built Robotic arm OR just 3D Printed components. The cost differs based on what option you choose.

Ages: 14-18

Hours: 6 hours a day – Five days a week

Schedule: Week of July 28 - 10 am to 5 pm (one hour break for lunch from 12 to 1 pm)

Price: $350 or $475

  • Regular Admission: $300 (fixed cost)
  • Robot motors/components - $50 if you take the 3D printed components or $175 if you take back the arm

Prerequisites: Some exposure to programming preferred but not required.

Tickets - Please contact us with details (name/age/prior experience) of the camper, and the option you want to pick and we will revert with a special online payment link.
