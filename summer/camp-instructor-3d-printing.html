About the Role

The Illinois MakerLab — the world's first business school 3D printing lab — is hiring a Summer Camp Instructor to lead our 3D printing and modeling camps for youth ages 10–17. This is a hands-on teaching and making role: you'll guide young campers through Minecraft builds, 3D modeling in TinkerCAD and Fusion 360, and real 3D printed creations they take home.
