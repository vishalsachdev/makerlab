Custom Keychains for MSBA Class of 2026

Published on Wed, 15 Oct 2025 by Illinois MakerLab

School pride meets custom fabrication! ReNae Pelmore from the Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) program at Gies College of Business worked with us to create custom Illinois-shaped keychains featuring the hashtag #MsbaAtGies and class year 2026.

The Design Process

Starting with our popular Illinois-shaped keychain template, we customized the design to include the MSBA program branding. The design challenge was finding the right font to display both uppercase and lowercase text clearly at a small scale:

  • Text needed to be legible on a keychain-sized print
  • Mixed case (#MsbaAtGies) required careful font selection
  • Orange filament to match Illinois branding

Our team modified the design in TinkerCAD, testing different fonts before finding the perfect balance of readability and style.

Batch Production

We printed 10 keychains in Illinois orange on printer #4, creating matching gifts for the cohort. These small branded items make excellent:

  • Welcome gifts for new students
  • Conference giveaways
  • Alumni keepsakes
  • Program promotional items

Custom Branded Items

Student organizations, academic programs, and campus departments regularly use the MakerLab for branded merchandise. From keychains to desk accessories, 3D printing offers a cost-effective way to create custom items in small quantities—perfect for special events or limited runs.

Interested in custom branded items for your program or organization? Contact us to discuss your ideas!
