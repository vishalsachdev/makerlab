# ChambanaMoms Summer Camp Promotion — Gold Plan

## What Is This?

We purchased the **Gold sponsorship ($550)** from [ChambanaMoms.com](https://chambanamoms.com) to promote our Summer 2026 camps to local families in the Champaign-Urbana area. The promotional season runs through **July 30, 2026**.

## What We Get (Gold Package)

1. **Listing in their summer camp guide** — our camps shown to all CU-area parents browsing for camps
2. **Secondary sponsorship of a summer in-person event** — our logo at a ChambanaMoms community event
3. **2 social media posts/stories** — posted on ChambanaMoms' Instagram/Facebook
4. **Inclusion in camp round-ups** — mentioned when they do "best summer camps" posts
5. **1 photo in their Facebook album** — in a summer camp album for the CU area
