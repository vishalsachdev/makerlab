Things we Make: Altgeld Hall

Published on Wed, 12 Feb 2014 by AricRindfleisch

In recent months, our Lab has received lots of requests to make things for various units across our campus. Most recently, the Illinois Geometry Lab asked us to print 20 copies (Orange & Blue) of Altgeld Hall. This is one of the most iconic buildings on our campus. If you would like your own copy of Altgeld Hall, just stop by our lab and we can print one for you! If you have models of other buildings that you would like to print, we'd love to add them to our collection as well.
