Any Early Preview of our New Home!

Published on Tue, 04 Jun 2013 by AricRindfleisch

Exciting news! Work has begun for our new home in the Business Instructional Facility. This new space will expand our workspace by 50%, provide us with greater visibility, and make our lab more accessible to Illinois students and faculty. Our anticipated move-in date is September 1 and we plan to have a Grand Opening Event in October. During the course of the summer, we will post updates on the renovation progress. Stay tuned!
