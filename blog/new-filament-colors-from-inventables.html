New Filament Colors from Inventables

Published on Sat, 12 Oct 2013 by AricRindfleisch

New colors are in! Our Lab has recently expanded its array of plastic filament to include silver, gold, brown, and neon orange! These colors, and many more, are available from Inventables, a company founded by University of Illinois alum, Zach Kaplan. In the coming weeks, we plan to expand our color palette even further and are open to requests from our maker community.
